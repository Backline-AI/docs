## What is Backline?

Backline is a comprehensive AI-powered security vulnerability and remediation management platform designed to streamline your organization's security workflow. By connecting your source control management (SCM) tools and security scanners, Backline automatically collects, analyzes, and remediates vulnerabilities across your entire organization.

## How Backline Works

1. **Connect** your SCM (GitHub, GitLab, Bitbucket) and security scanners
2. **Collect** vulnerabilities automatically from all connected sources
3. **Prioritize** based on severity, SLA, and impact
4. **Remediate** with AI-assisted fixes and automated pull requests
5. **Monitor** progress through comprehensive dashboards and reports
